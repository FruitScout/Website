Add-On Module · Photographic QA

Grade Every Cut. At Scale. In Real Time.

An A-Grade Jima cut delivers 15–22% higher ART sugar content than a C-Grade cut. FarmAgent grades every cut at full workforce scale — built into the Jima work ticket in the Field Agent app.

  1. Field Agent prompts the Jima worker to photograph the completed cut.
  2. Computer vision grades A, B, or C — cut angle, stub length, leaf trim, piña integrity — and returns the result before the worker moves on.
  3. Sub-standard cuts flag instantly in the Manager Hub QA Inbox — sorted by risk.
  4. One tap to confirm → rework ticket dispatched to the crew leader with the photo attached. The crew stays until it's resolved.
